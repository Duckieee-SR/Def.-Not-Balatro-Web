How to Play Balatro

In this special edition, we document the rules of the sensational new card game Balatro that's taking parlors and saloons by storm.

Gameplay: The player receives a previously approved set amount of cards. Choose which cards to play or discard by selecting them, then either play or discard the selected cards. New cards will be dealt to replace any discards or plays.
